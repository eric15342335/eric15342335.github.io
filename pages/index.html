About Me

I am an incoming year 4 undergraduate student at the University of Hong Kong, studying Applied Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science. Currently, I am working as a summer intern at FWD Group Management Holdings Limited, where I work under Group Technology and Digital (Group Technology Services), with my colleagues in the Finance Cloud Infrastructure and Operations team.

I am passionate about artificial intelligence technologies (automatic machine learning frameworks, computer vision, LLMs, model inference and deployment), cloud computing (like GCP and DigitalOcean that I've used before) and DevOps (CI/CD, IaC, large-scale distributed system management). The order of these interests does not indicate my proficiency or my interest level (In that case, you might stalk my GitHub starred repositories to better know what I really like!).

I am an open-source enthusiast. As a former member of the PyInstaller development team, I actively contributed hook patch requests to the pyinstaller-hooks-contrib repository, collaborating with developers such as bwoodsend and rokm.

For more information, please refer to my curriculum vitae.
