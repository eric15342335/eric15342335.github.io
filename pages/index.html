About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student at the University of Hong Kong, studying Applied Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science.

Previously, I studied linear statistical analysis (regression, ANOVA), database management systems (MySQL), image processing and computer vision, and web development (LAMP/Node/React) to build a solid foundation for future work in computer science and artificial intelligence. I also worked at Radio Television Hong Kong (RTHK) as a summer intern in Engineering Section (Broadcast Systems), Production Services Division. In year one, I worked at InspireLab Limited, where I developed embedded software for STEM education using an HKD$7 RISC-V microcontroller (CH32V003F4P6). My boss, Dr. Vincent Lau, and I attended the Hong Kong RISC-V Day + AI Con 2024 at the City University of Hong Kong to showcase the product.

I am an open-source enthusiast. As a former member of the PyInstaller development team, I actively contributed hook patch requests to the pyinstaller-hooks-contrib repository, collaborating with developers such as bwoodsend and rokm.

For more information, please refer to my curriculum vitae.
