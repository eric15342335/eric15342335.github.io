About Me

I am a second year undergraduate at the University of Hong Kong, studying Applied Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science.

Currently, I am studying linear statistical analysis (regression, ANOVA), database management systems (MySQL), image processing and computer vision, and web development (LAMP/Node/React) to build a solid foundation for future work in computer science and artificial intelligence. Previously, I worked at InspireLab Limited, where I developed embedded software for STEM education using an HKD$7 RISC-V microcontroller (CH32V003F4P6). My boss, Dr. Vincent Lau, and I attended the Hong Kong RISC-V Day + AI Con 2024 at the City University of Hong Kong to showcase the product.

I am an open-source enthusiast. As a former member of the PyInstaller development team, I actively contributed hook patch requests to the pyinstaller-hooks-contrib repository, collaborating with developers such as bwoodsend and rokm.

For more information, please refer to my curriculum vitae.
